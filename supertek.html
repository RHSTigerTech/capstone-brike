Information

I'm Michael Scalisi, the founder of Supertek Computers. I've been tinkering with computers for about four years now. Back in 2020, I got into building gaming computers. It all started when I helped my step-dad fix up his new computer. He showed me the ropes of swapping out parts and getting things running smoothly. About a year later, in 2021, I decided to start my own little computer company called Supertek. We started with a little owl logo. Since then, I've been fixing up computers under the Supertek name. My goal for Supertek is simple: I want to make custom gaming computers for gamers everywhere. I want them to have the chance to design their own machines, from picking the motherboard to the graphics card to the RAM, and even choosing the brand they prefer, like MSI or Asus. Looking ahead to 2024, I hope to have Supertek up and running smoothly, ready for people to buy computers from us.
